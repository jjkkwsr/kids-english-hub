## 🎯 學習目標 (Learning Target)

---

## 🔑 核心文法重點與句型 (Grammar Rules & Patterns)

### 1. Be-Verbs (am / is / are) 句型對照表

| 主詞 (Subject) | 肯定句 (Affirmative) | 否定句 (Negative) | 疑問句 (Yes/No Question) | 常用縮寫 (Contractions) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **I** | I **am** a student. | I **am not** a student. | **Am** I a student? | I'm / I'm not |
| **You** | You **are** a student. | You **are not** a student. | **Are** you a student? | You're / You aren't / You're not |
| **He / She / It** | She **is** a doctor. | She **is not** a doctor. | **Is** she a doctor? | She's / She isn't / She's not |
| **We / You / They** | They **are** students. | They **are not** students. | **Are** they students? | They're / They aren't / They're not |

---

### 2. There is / There are (存在句型)

| 類型 | 單數可數名詞 / 不可數名詞 | 複數名詞 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **肯定句 (Affirmative)** | There **is** a tree / some milk. | There **are** some apples. |
| **否定句 (Negative)** | There **isn't** a tree / any milk. | There **aren't** any apples. |
| **疑問句 (Question)** | **Is** there a tree / any milk? | **Are** there any apples? |

## 💡 針對 8 歲兒童的繁體中文 💡 Hint 導引與常見錯誤

### 💡 針對 8 歲兒童的 💡 Hint 導引庫

1. **I (我)** 的專屬搭配：
   - 💡 *Hint: 看到 "I" (我)，想一想搭配哪一個 Be 動詞呢？(am / am not)*
2. **單數 (He/She/It/一個人/一隻動物)**：
   - 💡 *Hint: 主詞是單數 (只有一個) 時，要搭配哪一個小動詞呢？(is / isn't)*
3. **複數 (We/They/多個人或動物/and)**：
   - 💡 *Hint: 「袋鼠和熊貓」加起來有兩個，算是複數 (They)，要用哪一個 Be 動詞呢？(are / aren't)*
4. **There is vs. There are**：
   - 💡 *Hint: 後面是一隻小狗 (單數)，前面要用 There is 還是 There are 呢？*
   - 💡 *Hint: 否定句或問句中，遇到複數名詞或不可數名詞要搭配 any 喔！*
