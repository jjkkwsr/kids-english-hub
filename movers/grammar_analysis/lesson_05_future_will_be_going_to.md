# Lesson 5: Future - Affirmatives & Negatives (will / be going to)

**冊別與層級**: Elementary Grammar Series - Level 3 (Movers Level - 8-Year-Old English)  
**類別**: Future Tense (未來時態)

---

## 🎯 學習目標 (Learning Target)
掌握未來式 will 與 be going to 的肯定與否定句型。

---

## 🔑 核心文法重點與句型 (Grammar Rules & Patterns)

### Future Tense (will / be going to)

| 類型 | 結構 | 範例 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **will 肯定** | Subject + **will** + V-base | I **will visit** my grandma tomorrow. |
| **will 否定** | Subject + **won't** + V-base | She **won't go** to school. (will not = won't) |
| **be going to 肯定** | Subject + **am/is/are going to** + V-base | We **are going to play** games. |
| **be going to 否定** | Subject + **am/is/are not going to** + V-base | He **isn't going to buy** a car. |


---

## 💡 針對 8 歲兒童的繁體中文 💡 Hint 導引與常見錯誤

### 💡 針對 8 歲兒童的 💡 Hint 導引庫

1. **will / won't 後面接原形動詞**：
   - 💡 *Hint: 看到 will 或 won't (將會/將不會)，後面的動詞要用最原始的形狀喔！*
2. **will not 的縮寫**：
   - 💡 *Hint: will + not 的縮寫不是 willn't，而是 won't 喔！*
