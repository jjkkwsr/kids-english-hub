# Lesson 6: Future - Yes/No Questions

**冊別與層級**: Elementary Grammar Series - Level 3 (Movers Level - 8-Year-Old English)  
**類別**: Future Tense (未來時態)

---

## 🎯 學習目標 (Learning Target)
學習未來式疑問句 (Will you...? / Are you going to...?) 的提問與回答。

---

## 🔑 核心文法重點與句型 (Grammar Rules & Patterns)

### Future Questions & Answers

| 提問方式 | 句型結構 | 簡答 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Will 疑問句** | **Will** + Subject + V-base...? | Yes, I **will**. / No, I **won't**. |
| **Be going to 疑問句** | **Am/Is/Are** + Subject + **going to** + V-base...? | Yes, she **is**. / No, she **isn't**. |


---

## 💡 針對 8 歲兒童的繁體中文 💡 Hint 導引與常見錯誤

### 💡 針對 8 歲兒童的 💡 Hint 導引庫

1. **Will 開頭的問句**：
   - 💡 *Hint: 用 Will 提問，回答也要用 will 或 won't 喔！*
2. **Are you going to...? 的回答**：
   - 💡 *Hint: 問句是 "Are you going to..." 時，回答要變成 "Yes, I am." 或 "No, I'm not." 喔！*
