## 🔑 核心文法重點與句型 (Grammar Rules & Patterns)

### Regular Verbs Past Simple (-ed)

1. 一般動詞：+ `-ed` (walk $\to$ walked, play $\to$ played)
2. 字尾有 `e`：直接 + `-d` (like $\to$ liked, live $\to$ lived)
3. 字尾為 子音 + y：去 `y` 改 `-ied` (cry $\to$ cried, study $\to$ studied)
4. 短母音+單子音：雙寫字尾 + `-ed` (stop $\to$ stopped, hop $\to$ hopped)
