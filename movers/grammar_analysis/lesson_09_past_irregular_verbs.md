---

## 🎯 學習目標 (Learning Target)

---

## 🔑 核心文法重點與句型 (Grammar Rules & Patterns)

### Common Irregular Verbs (常用不規則動詞表)

| 原形動詞 (Base) | 過去式 (Past) | 範例 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **go** | **went** | We **went** to the zoo yesterday. |
| **have / has** | **had** | They **had** a good time. |
| **see** | **saw** | I **saw** a dolphin in the pool. |
| **eat** | **ate** | He **ate** a sandwich. |
| **make** | **made** | She **made** a cake. |
| **buy** | **bought** | We **bought** a ticket. |
| **run** | **ran** | The dog **ran** fast. |
| **take** | **took** | He **took** the bus. |
