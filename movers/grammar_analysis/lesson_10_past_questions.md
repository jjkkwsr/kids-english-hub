## 🎯 學習目標 (Learning Target)

---

## 🔑 核心文法重點與句型 (Grammar Rules & Patterns)

### Past Simple Questions & Negatives

| 類型 | 結構 | 範例 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **否定句** | Subject + **didn't** + V-base | I **didn't see** the clown. (did not = didn't) |
| **疑問句** | **Did** + Subject + V-base...? | **Did** you **go** to the park? |
| **簡答** | Yes, I **did**. / No, I **didn't**. | |
