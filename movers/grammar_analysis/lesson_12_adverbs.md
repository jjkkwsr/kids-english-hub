---

## 🔑 核心文法重點與句型 (Grammar Rules & Patterns)

### Adverbs of Manner (形容詞轉副詞規則)

1. 一般形容詞 + `-ly`：quick $\to$ **quickly**, quiet $\to$ **quietly**, loud $\to$ **loudly**
2. 字尾為 y $\to$ 去 y 改 `-ily`：happy $\to$ **happily**, easy $\to$ **easily**
3. 不規則變形：good $\to$ **well**, fast $\to$ **fast**, hard $\to$ **hard**
