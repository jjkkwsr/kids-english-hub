---

## 🔑 核心文法重點與句型 (Grammar Rules & Patterns)

### Comparatives (-er + than)

| 規則 | 形容詞 | 比較級 | 句型範例 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **單音節 + er** | tall / fast | **taller / faster** | A lion is **taller than** a cat. |
| **字尾 y 去 y + ier** | happy / funny | **happier / funnier** | The clown is **funnier than** the pirate. |
| **三音節以上 + more** | beautiful | **more beautiful** | The parrot is **more beautiful than** the bat. |
