---

## 🔑 核心文法重點與句型 (Grammar Rules & Patterns)

### Superlatives (the + -est)

1. 單音節：**the + adj-est** (the tallest, the fastest)
2. 字尾 y：**the + adj-iest** (the happiest, the funniest)
3. 長字：**the most + adj** (the most beautiful)
