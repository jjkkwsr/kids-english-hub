# Lesson 18: Prepositions of Place and Time (介系詞)

**冊別與層級**: Elementary Grammar Series - Level 3 (Movers Level - 8-Year-Old English)  
**類別**: Prepositions & Modals (介系詞與助動詞)

---

## 🎯 學習目標 (Learning Target)
學習地點介系詞 (in, on, under, next to, behind, in front of, between) 與時間介系詞 (at, on, in)。

---

## 🔑 核心文法重點與句型 (Grammar Rules & Patterns)

### 1. Prepositions of Place (地點介系詞)
- **in** (在...裡面), **on** (在...上面), **under** (在...下面), **next to** (在...旁邊), **behind** (在...後面), **in front of** (在...前面), **between** (在...之間)

### 2. Prepositions of Time (時間介系詞)
- **at** + 具體時間/時刻 (at 7 o'clock, at night)
- **on** + 具體日期/星期 (on Sunday, on July 1st)
- **in** + 月份/年份/季節/時段 (in July, in summer, in the morning)
