## 🔑 核心文法重點與句型 (Grammar Rules & Patterns)

### Modal Verbs (can / must / should)

1. **can**：能力或請求 (I **can** swim.)
2. **must**：強烈必須/規定 (You **must** be quiet.)
3. **should**：建議或忠告 (You **should** drink warm tea.)

⚠️ **核心規則**：Modal Verb + **V-base (原形動詞)**
