## 🔑 核心文法重點與句型 (Grammar Rules & Patterns)

### Wh-Question Words

- **Who** (誰 - 人) $\to$ **Who** is that man?
- **What** (什麼 - 事物/職業) $\to$ **What** is your name?
- **Where** (在哪裡 - 地點) $\to$ **Where** is the library?
- **When** (什麼時候 - 時間) $\to$ **When** is your birthday?
- **Why** (為什麼 - 原因) $\to$ **Why** are you crying?
- **How** (如何/怎樣 - 方式/狀態) $\to$ **How** do you go to school?
