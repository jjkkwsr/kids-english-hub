🔑 TEACHER'S ANSWER KEY (教師解答卷)

Lesson 1 Pattern Practice Answer Key: Be-Verbs & Toy Shop

Challenge 1: 角色的身份與裝備 (示範與參考造句)
• 參考造句 1: I am a funny clown with a red nose.
• 參考造句 2: I am a happy farmer with a green farm.
Challenge 2: 看到兩隻動物 (示範與參考造句)
• 參考造句 1: Look! There is a soft white rabbit and a tiny kitten.
• 參考造句 2: Look! There is a smart blue dolphin and a giant grey whale.
Challenge 3: 物品在哪裡 (示範與參考造句)
• 參考造句 1: My warm socks are on my feet.
• 參考造句 2: My red gloves are on my hands.
Challenge 4: 特質對比 (示範與參考造句)
• 參考造句 1: He isn't slow; he is very fast!
• 參考造句 2: She isn't sad; she is very happy!
