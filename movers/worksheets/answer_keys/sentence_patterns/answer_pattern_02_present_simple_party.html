🔑 TEACHER'S ANSWER KEY (教師解答卷)

Lesson 2 Pattern Practice Answer Key: Present Simple & The Party

Challenge 1: 單數主詞的習慣動作 (示範與參考造句)
• 參考造句 1: My dad cooks hot soup every evening.
• 參考造句 2: Uncle Ben drinks orange juice every morning.
Challenge 2: 複數主詞的否定對比 (示範與參考造句)
• 參考造句 1: We don't write letters; we write phone messages.
• 參考造句 2: They don't play tennis; they play football.
Challenge 3: 餐桌放置動作 (示範與參考造句)
• 參考造句 1: The kind grandma puts yellow cheese on a white plate.
• 參考造句 2: The happy son puts warm milk in a small cup.
Challenge 4: 複數人物動作躲藏 (示範與參考造句)
• 參考造句 1: The cute kittens don't make noise; they sleep under the sofa.
• 參考造句 2: The brother and sister don't sleep; they read books behind the green tree.
