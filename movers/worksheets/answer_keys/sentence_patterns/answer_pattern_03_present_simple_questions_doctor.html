🔑 TEACHER'S ANSWER KEY (教師解答卷)

Lesson 3 Pattern Practice Answer Key: Present Simple Questions & Doctor

Challenge 1: 單數主詞的症狀問答 (示範與參考造句)
• 參考造句 1: Does your brother have a bad stomachache? — Yes, he does!
• 參考造句 2: Does Lily have a bad earache? — Yes, she does!
Challenge 2: 詢問感覺與動作 (示範與參考造句)
• 參考造句 1: Do you feel sore in your back when you carry a heavy bag?
• 參考造句 2: Do you hurt your shoulder when you play tennis?
Challenge 3: 醫護檢查動作 (示範與參考造句)
• 參考造句 1: The kind nurse checks my temperature with a thermometer.
• 參考造句 2: The tall doctor checks my ears with a small light.
Challenge 4: 意願與休息需求 (示範與參考造句)
• 參考造句 1: She doesn't like to take bitter medicine; she wants to drink warm water.
• 參考造句 2: The tired patient doesn't like to lie in bed all day; he wants to rest under the tree.
