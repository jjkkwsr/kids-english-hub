🔑 TEACHER'S ANSWER KEY (教師解答卷)

Lesson 1 Story Quest Answer Key: The Magic Toy Shop

Story 1: The Magic Toy Shop
Hello! I am Tim. Today, I am a brave pirate with a big pirate ship.
I am in Mr. Jolly's Magic Toy Shop. Look! There is a tall hopping kangaroo and a small black-and-white panda. They are jumping on the floor!
Look up at the high roof! There is a funny clown with a red nose. He isn't quiet; he is very loud! Next to him, a green parrot says, "Hello!"
Oh, where are my shoes? My roller skates are on my feet. I am ready to skate home!
Story 2: The Sunny Farm & Pet Corner
My uncle is a happy farmer. He works on a big farm with green fields.
Is there a small barking puppy on the grass? Yes, there is! A soft white long-eared rabbit and a tiny meowing kitten are playing together. Nearby, a brown bird is sleeping in a wooden cage.
Are there any scary wolves on the farm? No, there aren't!
I am not scared of the animals. Under the tree, a black flying bat is sleeping. I sit on the bench and read my favorite comic book. What a fun day!
Story 3: The Ocean World & Safari Park
Welcome to Ocean World! There is a smart swimming dolphin in the blue pool. It isn't slow; it is super fast!
Look over there! There is a giant grey whale in the deep ocean. Is there a scary shark with sharp teeth? No, there isn't!
Next to the pool, a wild roaring lion is standing under the tree. He is tall and strong. I love reading my comic about animals today!
