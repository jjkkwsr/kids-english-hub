🔑 TEACHER'S ANSWER KEY (教師解答卷)

Lesson 2 Story Quest Answer Key: The Party

Story 1: The Big Family Party
Today is a happy day! My kind grandparent and grandmother arrive at our house. Every grown-up at the party smiles and talks loudly.
My dad is a loving parent. He goes to the kitchen and makes hot drinks. My mom watches TV in the living room. She doesn't like noisy games!
Look at the children! Uncle Ben plays with his young son and his little daughter. They have bright blond hair. Grandpa is very proud of his brave grandson and sweet granddaughter. Everyone lives happily in this house!
Story 2: Fun at the Dinner Table
It is dinner time! Mom puts a deep bowl of hot vegetable soup on the wooden table. My little baby brother cries when the soup is too hot!
Dad has a slice of yellow cheese on his white plate. He drinks juice from a clear glass bottle. I don't drink coffee; I have a small cup of warm milk!
Grandma pours juice into a tall glass for me. Everyone has a great appetite tonight!
Story 3: Hide and Seek in the Garden
After dinner, we play a fun game outside! My sister hides behind the big green tree. She is very quiet; she doesn't make any sound.
Look up! A colorful bird flies high over the garden. It doesn't like to stay inside the wooden cage. It carries a secret message on its leg.
We love to hide in the dark garden. Dad sends a text message to tell us it is time to come inside!
