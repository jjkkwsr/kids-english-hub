🔑 TEACHER'S ANSWER KEY (教師解答卷)

Lesson 3 Story Quest Answer Key: At the Doctor's

Story 1: A Visit to the Friendly Doctor
Today, Tim feels unwell. Does he have a high fever? Yes, he does! He goes to the clinic with his mom.
A friendly doctor with a thick black moustache smiles at him. The doctor asks, "Do you have a sore throat?" Tim nods slowly.
The kind nurse wraps a warm scarf around Tim's neck. Tim has a bad cough and a runny nose. The doctor says, "He doesn't need to worry; he just has a common cold!"
Story 2: The Afternoon Clinic
At 2 o'clock, two children wait in the clinic. Do you feel sick today? Yes, we do!
Little Sam has a sharp stomachache in his belly. Does his stomach feel full and sore? Yes, it does!
His sister Lily has a painful headache from studying all day. Her upper back feels sore, and she has a dull backache after carrying a heavy bag. Her left shoulder also feels stiff.
The nurse checks Lily's temperature with a thermometer. "You don't have a high fever," says the nurse.
Story 3: A Brave Little Patient at the Hospital
Jimmy goes to the big city hospital with his mother. Does he feel sick? Yes, he does!
Jimmy loves to climb high trees in the park. But today, he feels very tired and wants to rest.
Does he have a bad earache after swimming? Yes, he does! Does he have a sharp toothache from eating candies? No, he doesn't!
Does Jimmy cry when the doctor checks his ears? No! His ears hurt a little bit, but he is a super brave boy!
