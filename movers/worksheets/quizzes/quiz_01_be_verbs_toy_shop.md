### Part 2: Speaking & Read Aloud Task (單字朗讀與句型任務 🗣️)

請大聲朗讀以下句子，**完整覆蓋 Lesson 1 所有 21 個重點單字**，並在朗讀時圈出句中的 **Be 動詞 (am / is / are / was / were)**：

1. The **clown** and the **pirate** are jumping on the **farm**.  *(單字: clown, pirate, farm)*
2. A **tall** **farmer** is looking at the **kangaroo** and the **panda**.  *(單字: tall, farmer, kangaroo, panda)*
3. The cute **puppy** and the small **kitten** are playing with a **rabbit**.  *(單字: puppy, kitten, rabbit)*
4. Look! A green **parrot** is sitting on the **cage**, and a black **bat** is under the **roof**.  *(單字: parrot, cage, bat, roof)*
5. The smart **dolphin** and the big grey **whale** are swimming away from the scary **shark**.  *(單字: dolphin, whale, shark)*
6. I have a fun **comic** (or **comic book**) and a pair of fast **roller skates**.  *(單字: comic, comic book, roller skates, lion)*
