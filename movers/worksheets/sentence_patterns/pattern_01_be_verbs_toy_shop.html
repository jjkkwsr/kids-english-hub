Challenge 1: 角色的身份與裝備 (I am a...)
📌 示範句子 (Model Sentence):
I am a brave pirate with a big ship.
🎁 造句素材寶盒 (可自由選擇組合):
【形容詞】: funny happy kind tall
【職業/角色】: clown farmer doctor
【裝備/物品】: red nose green farm small clinic
✍️ 請照樣造句 (Your New Sentence):
I am a with a .
💡 結構提示：I am a [形容詞] [角色] with a [形容詞] [物品]. (我是一個...的...，帶著一個...)
Challenge 2: 看到兩隻動物 (Look! There is... and...)
📌 示範句子 (Model Sentence):
Look! There is a tall kangaroo and a small panda.
🎁 造句素材寶盒 (可自由選擇組合):
【形容詞】: soft white tiny smart blue giant grey
【動物1】: rabbit puppy dolphin
【動物2】: kitten bat whale
✍️ 請照樣造句 (Your New Sentence):
Look! There is a and a .
💡 結構提示：Look! There is a [形容詞] [動物1] and a [形容詞] [動物2]. (看！那裡有一隻...和一隻...)
Challenge 3: 物品在哪裡 (My... are on my...)
📌 示範句子 (Model Sentence):
My roller skates are on my feet.
🎁 造句素材寶盒 (可自由選擇組合):
【複數物品】: warm socks black shoes red gloves
【身體部位】: feet hands head
✍️ 請照樣造句 (Your New Sentence):
My are on my .
💡 結構提示：My [複數物品] are on my [身體部位]. (我的...在我的...上面。)
Challenge 4: 特質對比 (He/She isn't...; he/she is very...)
📌 示範句子 (Model Sentence):
He isn't quiet; he is very loud!
🎁 造句素材寶盒 (可自由選擇組合):
【主詞】: He She The clown
【相反形容詞組】: slow / fast small / big sad / happy
✍️ 請照樣造句 (Your New Sentence):
isn't ; is very !
💡 結構提示：[人物] isn't [形容詞1]; [代名詞] is very [形容詞2]! (他/她不...；他/她非常...！)
