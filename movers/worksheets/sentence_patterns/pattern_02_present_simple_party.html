Challenge 2: 複數主詞的否定對比 (We don't...; we...)
📌 示範句子 (Model Sentence):
We don't drink coffee; we drink orange juice.
🎁 造句素材寶盒 (可自由選擇組合):
【主詞】: We They My parents
【否定動作】: don't write letters don't play tennis don't eat meat
【肯定動作】: write phone messages play football eat fresh salad
✍️ 請照樣造句 (Your New Sentence):
; .
💡 結構提示：[複數主詞] don't [動詞1] [物品1]; [複數主詞] [動詞2] [物品2]. (我們不...；我們...)
Challenge 3: 餐桌放置動作 (The... puts... in/on...)
📌 示範句子 (Model Sentence):
The quiet boy puts hot soup in a big bowl.
🎁 造句素材寶盒 (可自由選擇組合):
【形容詞+人物】: kind grandma happy son polite daughter
【食物/飲料】: yellow cheese warm milk fresh juice
【容器】: white plate small cup glass bottle
✍️ 請照樣造句 (Your New Sentence):
The puts in/on a .
💡 結構提示：The [形容詞+人物] puts [食物] in/on a [容器]. (這位...把...放在...裡/上。)
Challenge 4: 複數人物動作躲藏 (The... don't...; they... under...)
📌 示範句子 (Model Sentence):
The children don't cry; they hide under the tree.
🎁 造句素材寶盒 (可自由選擇組合):
【複數人物/動物】: brother and sister cute kittens puppies
【不做的動作】: make noise sleep
【做的動作】: play games read books hide
【地點】: roof sofa green tree
✍️ 請照樣造句 (Your New Sentence):
The don't ; they under/behind the .
💡 結構提示：The [複數對象] don't [動詞1]; they [動詞2] under/behind the [地點].
